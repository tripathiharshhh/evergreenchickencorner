Our Story

Where Every Bite Tells a Story

Tucked in the heart of Sector 57, Gurugram, Ever Green Chicken Corner brings the royal Mughal culinary tradition to your table — at prices that make you come back every week. A neighbourhood favourite for kebabs, tandoori grills and fresh rolls.

🔥

Fresh Off the Tandoor

Every kebab and tikka is hand-marinated with house spices and roasted in a live clay tandoor for authentic smoky depth.

🌿

Quality Ingredients

Fresh produce and quality meats are sourced daily to ensure every dish meets our high standard of taste and hygiene.

💰

Pocket-Friendly Prices

Royal flavours don't have to cost a fortune. Enjoy a full meal for two at just ₹400 — great value in Gurugram.
